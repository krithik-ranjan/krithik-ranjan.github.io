Hello! I am Krithik Ranjan

I'm a senior at Cornell University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering with a minor in Design + Environmental Analysis.
I am passionate about increasing the impact of technology among marginalized communities of people with disabilities, the elderly, and resource poor environments. I am interested in rethinking how we use and create with computers to develop intuitive and low-cost modalities of human-computer interaction that enable more people to explore their creativity through technology.

This page showcases some of my non-research projects.
